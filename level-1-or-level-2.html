What Is a Level 1 Condition Report?

A Level 1 survey is the most basic RICS survey available. It provides:

  • A visual inspection of the property's main elements
  • Traffic light ratings for condition (green, amber, red)
  • Identification of urgent or significant issues
  • Note: No advice on repairs or ongoing maintenance

Best For:

  • New-builds or nearly new properties
  • Homes with no visible defects
  • Buyers who just want a general snapshot before proceeding

What Is a Level 2 HomeBuyer Report?

A Level 2 survey is the most commonly chosen report for conventional homes. It includes everything in Level 1, plus:

  • Commentary on the condition of key building elements
  • Notes on repairs, risks, and potential hidden defects
  • Advice on maintenance and next steps

Best For:

  • Properties built after 1900
  • Homes that appear in decent condition but haven’t been recently renovated
  • Buyers who want a clearer understanding of the building’s performance
