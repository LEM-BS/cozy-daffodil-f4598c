Surveying Services in Tarvin

  • RICS Level 1 Home Survey: Basic condition check of newer or well-maintained properties. Traffic light ratings only — no advice.
  • RICS Level 2 Home Survey: Ideal for most standard homes — includes condition ratings, maintenance advice, and repair recommendations.
  • RICS Level 3 Building Survey: The most detailed report for older, extended, or non-standard homes — includes structure, defects, repair options, and optional costings.
  • Damp & Timber Reports: Independent assessments for moisture, mould, or decay issues — ideal for mortgage lenders or peace of mind.
  • Measured Surveys & Floorplans: Accurate internal plans for extensions, planning or space layout.
  • EPCs with Floorplans: Energy assessments plus marketing-ready layout plans — perfect for lettings and sales listings.
  • Ventilation Assessments: Identify condensation, mould or poor airflow risks and recommend appropriate improvements.
