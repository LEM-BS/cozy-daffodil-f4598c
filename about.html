Who We Are

LEM Building Surveying Ltd is owned and run by Liam Butler BSc AssocRICS MRPSA, an independent surveyor serving Deeside, Chester, Flintshire and the wider North West.

Liam brings a personal, hands-on approach to every instruction—combining technical knowledge with calm, practical reporting that helps you make informed decisions about your property.

Meet Liam

Liam holds a BSc in Building Surveying and is an Associate Member of RICS and a Member of the Residential Property Surveyors Association (MRPSA).

With over seven years’ dedicated surveying experience, he’s inspected everything from Victorian terraces and pre-1930s homes to modern apartments and timber-frame builds. His strengths lie in defect diagnosis, energy-efficiency reporting and providing plain-English advice without alarmism.

Why Choose Us?

  • Truly Independent: No estate-agent ties or repair sales—just impartial advice based on what’s best for you.
  • Fast Turnaround: Reports typically delivered within 48–72 hours of inspection.
  • Local Knowledge: Deep insight into local housing—from shallow sandstone footings common in Chester terraces to clay-movement risk zones in Flintshire.
  • Friendly & Honest: Clients often comment on Liam’s reassuring, straightforward approach—especially when urgent defects are flagged.
