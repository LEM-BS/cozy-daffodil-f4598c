Our Services

  • RICS Level 1 Home Survey: A basic report highlighting key visible issues. Ideal for new-build or conventionally built homes in good condition.
  • RICS Level 2 Home Survey (Homebuyer Report): A detailed visual inspection of all major elements, covering significant defects, maintenance guidance, and repair advice.
  • RICS Level 3 Home Survey (Building Survey): The most comprehensive option, perfect for older, extended, or non-standard properties. Includes structural concerns and tailored recommendations.
  • Damp & Mould Surveys: Identify causes of dampness, mould, condensation, and moisture—backed by impartial advice and digital readings.
  • Damp & Timber Reports: Inspection and reporting on woodworm, rot, and associated damp issues, ensuring structural safety.
  • Residential Ventilation Assessments: Ensuring healthy air quality and identifying poor ventilation in homes.
