Measured Building Surveys & Floorplans

Accurate, professional property measurement and floorplans for renovations, extensions, and documentation.

Request a Survey

Our Measured Survey Services

  • 2D Floorplans: Clear, precise layouts of property interiors—ideal for renovation planning, property marketing, or lease agreements.
  • Elevations & Sections: Accurate external and internal views supporting detailed planning applications or structural assessments.
  • Site Plans: Detailed property and boundary measurements crucial for development, planning permissions, and records.

Who Benefits from Our Surveys

  • Homeowners: Essential for accurate planning of extensions, renovations, or interior design.
  • Landlords: Clear, professional floorplans to document layouts, assist with lease agreements, and property management.
  • Property Investors: Precise measurements to evaluate opportunities, assess alterations, and make informed decisions.

Why Choose LEM Building Surveying?

  • Tailored Service: Personalised survey solutions matching your property's unique characteristics and project goals.
  • Local Expertise: Familiarity with regional building types, layouts, and planning requirements across the North West.
  • Clear Deliverables: Professionally presented, easy-to-understand plans and reports that communicate key information clearly.
