Common Issues Identified

  • Rising Damp: Moisture rising from the ground through walls, potentially damaging finishes and structure.
  • Penetrating Damp: Water ingress through roofs, walls, windows, or defective flashings.
  • Condensation & Mould: Unhealthy mould growth and condensation due to poor ventilation or insulation.
  • Woodworm Infestation: Insect activity in timbers that can weaken structural elements.
  • Wet & Dry Rot: Fungal decay affecting wood, causing serious structural weakness if left untreated.
