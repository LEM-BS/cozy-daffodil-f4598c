Property Surveys in Chester

  • RICS Level 1 Survey: A basic condition report for newer, well-maintained homes — no repair advice, just key findings and ratings.
  • RICS Level 2 Survey: The most common survey for buyers — a HomeBuyer Report with clear commentary, repair risks, and advice.
  • RICS Level 3 Survey: A full Building Survey suitable for older or extended properties — includes detailed structural commentary and repair options.
  • Damp & Timber Reports: Independent diagnosis of mould, rising damp, condensation and decay — no sales pitch, just facts.
  • Measured Surveys & Floorplans: Scaled internal plans and dimensions for extensions, planning, or documentation.
  • EPCs with Floorplans: Required for property sales or rentals — includes a marketing-ready floorplan.
  • Ventilation Assessments: Identify airflow, humidity or mould risk issues before they become a problem.
