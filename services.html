Our Services

RICS Home Surveys

We provide three levels of surveys based on RICS Home Survey Standards, each designed for different property types and client requirements.

Level 1 – RICS Condition Report

A basic overview that identifies urgent or significant defects.

Level 2 – RICS HomeBuyer Report

A mid-level inspection offering detailed analysis and practical maintenance advice.

Level 3 – RICS Building Survey

A comprehensive survey for older, larger, or more complex properties detailing structural integrity and repair needs.

Damp Surveys

Our damp surveys detect and assess moisture-related issues to prevent long-term damage.

Measured Surveys and Floorplans

Accurate surveys and detailed floorplans for renovations, extensions, or professional documentation.

EPCs with Floorplans

Our combined EPC services, with integrated floorplans, provide a comprehensive view of energy efficiency and property layout.

Residential Ventilation Assessments

Assessments to evaluate and ensure your property's ventilation meets required standards.
