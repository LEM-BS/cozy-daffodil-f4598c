Our Professional Surveying Services

At LEM Building Surveying Ltd, we offer a comprehensive range of property surveying services to assist you in making informed decisions about your investments. Our expertise ensures you receive accurate and reliable assessments tailored to your specific needs.

Residential Surveys

RICS Level 1 Home Survey
A basic condition report suitable for newer properties, highlighting any immediate issues.

RICS Level 2 Home Survey
A mid-level report providing a detailed assessment of the property's condition, ideal for standard homes.

RICS Level 3 Home Survey
A comprehensive structural survey for older or complex properties, offering in-depth analysis of structural integrity.

Specialist Reports

Damp & Timber Reports
Expert analysis identifying issues related to moisture, rot, and timber decay, helping prevent long-term structural damage.

Fire Safety Assessments
Evaluations of fire risks and passive fire protection measures to ensure compliance and safety.

Ventilation Assessments
Inspections to ensure residential ventilation systems comply with Building Regulations Part F, promoting healthy indoor air quality.
