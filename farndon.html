Home Survey Options

  • Level 1 — Condition Report: Basic summary of condition, no advice — best for newer homes with no obvious defects.
  • Level 2 — HomeBuyer Survey: The most popular choice for standard homes — includes repair advice and maintenance tips.
  • Level 3 — Building Survey: Full structural inspection for older or extended homes — ideal for cottages, stone homes, or non-standard construction.
  • Damp & Timber Reports: For targeted damp issues, rot or timber defects.
  • EPCs & Floorplans: Useful for listings, compliance, or planning alterations.
