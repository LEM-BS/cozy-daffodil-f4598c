How Much Does a Level 3 Survey Cost in Chester?

Typical costs, what influences the price, and why it’s worth the investment.

If you're buying a property in Chester and need a comprehensive inspection, a RICS Level 3 Building Survey offers the deepest analysis. But how much should you expect to pay?

Average Level 3 Survey Fees in Chester

For most homes in Chester, Level 3 survey prices start from around £700 and can exceed £1,200 for large or complex properties. Factors that affect the fee include:

  • Size and age of the property
  • Construction type and any extensions
  • Specific issues such as damp, timber decay, or structural movement

Why Costs Vary

A period townhouse in the city centre requires more time than a modern terrace. At LEM Building Surveying, we tailor each quote to the property so you only pay for the time needed.

Value for Money

A Level 3 survey can reveal hidden defects that might cost thousands to fix. Investing in a detailed report now can give you leverage to renegotiate or budget for repairs.
