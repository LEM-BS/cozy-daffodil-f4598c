What Are RICS Home Surveys?

RICS Home Surveys are professional inspections designed to assess the condition of a property before purchase. Whether you’re a first-time buyer or an experienced investor, these surveys help you understand potential issues and make informed decisions.

RICS Survey Levels

We provide three levels of surveys based on RICS Home Survey Standards:

Level 1 – RICS Condition Report

A basic overview that identifies urgent or significant defects. Ideal for modern properties in generally good condition.

Level 2 – RICS HomeBuyer Report

A mid-level inspection that offers a detailed analysis, including an assessment of structural elements and practical maintenance advice.

Level 3 – RICS Building Survey

A comprehensive survey for older, larger, or more complex properties. This in-depth report details structural integrity and repair requirements.

How to Choose the Right Survey

Property Age & Condition:

For newer homes, a Level 1 may suffice. Older or mixed-age properties often need a more detailed report.

Extent of Work Needed:

If you suspect hidden issues or visible defects, a Level 3 survey can uncover crucial details.

Your Investment Strategy:

A thorough survey can save time and money if you plan renovations or require maintenance insights.
