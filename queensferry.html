Our Property Survey Services

  • RICS Level 1 – Condition Report: Covers overall condition of newer homes, using a simple traffic-light system to flag any concerns.

  • RICS Level 3 – Building Survey: Full inspection and reporting for older or altered homes, including structural observations and advice.
