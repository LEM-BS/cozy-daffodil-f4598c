Survey Options Available in Flintshire

  • RICS Level 1 Home Survey: For newer homes in good condition — a basic overview of key risks and visible issues.
  • RICS Level 2 Home Survey: A mid-level HomeBuyer Report including repairs, defects and maintenance advice.
  • RICS Level 3 Building Survey: A comprehensive report for older, extended, or unusual properties — including construction, condition and repair options.
  • Damp & Timber Reports: Find the cause of damp, mould or decay and get clear, reliable guidance on resolving it.
  • Measured Surveys & Floorplans: Precise layouts for planning, extensions, or property records.
  • EPCs with Floorplans: Combined Energy Performance Certificate and marketing-ready layout plans.
  • Ventilation Assessments: Ideal for condensation and indoor air quality concerns.
