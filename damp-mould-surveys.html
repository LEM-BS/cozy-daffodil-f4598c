Common Issues Assessed

  • Condensation & Mould: Caused by poor ventilation, excess moisture, or inadequate heating.
  • Rising Damp: Moisture rising from the ground through walls, potentially causing structural damage.
  • Penetrating Damp: Water ingress through roofs, walls, or windows.
  • Inadequate Ventilation: Poor airflow leading to condensation and unhealthy indoor environments.
  • High Indoor Humidity: Persistent dampness and mould growth due to elevated humidity.

Who This Service Helps

  • Homeowners: Mould patches, damp walls, or musty odours.
  • Landlords: Compliant, impartial reports for tenant concerns or housing regulations.
  • Buyers: Reassurance before purchasing a property with visible or suspected damp issues.
  • Social Housing Providers: Independent assessments for housing stock management or disrepair claims.

What You’ll Receive

  • Detailed On-site Inspection: Moisture and humidity readings taken directly at your property.
  • Ventilation & Condensation Assessment: Review of extractor fans, air circulation, and condensation risks.
  • Clear, Comprehensive Report: Easy-to-understand findings, photographic evidence, and practical recommendations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes—our detailed reports are suitable for landlord-tenant disputes and compliance purposes.

Absolutely. We assess all relevant ventilation systems thoroughly.

We can suggest types of specialists but have no commercial affiliations, ensuring unbiased recommendations.
