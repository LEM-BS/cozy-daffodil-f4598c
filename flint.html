Our Surveying Services

  • RICS Level 1 Survey: A basic condition check ideal for newer properties with no obvious issues. Provides colour-coded ratings but no repair advice.
  • RICS Level 2 Survey: A popular choice for most homes — includes condition ratings, repair advice, and maintenance tips.
  • RICS Level 3 Survey: A full Building Survey for older or extended homes — includes structural concerns, repair options, and detailed analysis.
  • Damp & Timber Reports: Independent investigation of mould, damp patches or timber decay — perfect for peace of mind or lender requirements.
  • Measured Surveys & Floorplans: Internal layout drawings for renovation, design or marketing use.
  • EPCs with Floorplans: Energy certificate plus floorplan — ideal for letting or selling properties.
  • Ventilation Assessments: Helps identify causes of condensation and poor airflow affecting indoor air quality.
