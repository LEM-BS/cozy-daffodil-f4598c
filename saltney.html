RICS Survey Services

  • Level 1 — Condition Report: Suitable for newer or well-maintained properties. Covers general condition using a traffic light system.
  • Level 2 — HomeBuyer Report: A mid-range survey ideal for most conventional properties. Includes repair advice and recommendations.
  • Level 3 — Building Survey: A detailed inspection and report for older or extended homes — includes structure, materials, defects and repair guidance.
  • Damp & Timber Surveys: Pinpoint damp, mould or rot issues with expert diagnosis and next steps.
  • EPCs with Floorplans: Energy assessments with internal layout plans — perfect for sales or lettings.
